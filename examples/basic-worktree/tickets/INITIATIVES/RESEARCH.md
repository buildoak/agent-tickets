## Objective

Turn broad questions into grounded briefs with sources, tradeoffs, and next
actions.

## Scope Pattern

A good research card names:
- the question;
- what sources count;
- what comparison or output is expected;
- what “done” means.

## Done Criteria

Research tickets are done when `## Result` includes findings, evidence,
blocked items, and the recommended next step.

## Conventions

- Prefer primary sources.
- Cite URLs or local file paths used as evidence.
- Separate observation from inference.
- Keep the result compact enough for the coordinator to act on.
